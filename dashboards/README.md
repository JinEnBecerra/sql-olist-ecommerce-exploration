## Dashboards Overview  

This project explores Brazilian e-commerce data from 2016 to 2018, focusing on overall business performance, customer experience, and key revenue drivers.

---

### 1. Business Performance  
**Focus:**  
- Monthly Revenue Growth (2016–2018)  
- Average Order Value (AOV) Trend  
- Monthly Order Cancellation Rate  
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)  

**Goal:**  
Provide a high-level view of overall business health and performance trends over time.

---

### 2. Delivery & Customer Experience  
**Focus:**  
- Delivery Time Trend  
- Late Delivery Rate Over Time  
- Review Score Trend  
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)  

**Goal:**  
Evaluate customer satisfaction and delivery performance to identify potential operational issues.

---

### 3. Revenue Drivers  
**Focus:**  
- Revenue Contribution by State  
- Top Product Categories by Revenue  
- Top 5 Sellers by Revenue  
- High-Sales but Low-Rated Product Categories  
- Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)  

**Goal:**  
Identify the main contributors to revenue and uncover opportunities for business optimization.
